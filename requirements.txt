**How to get your Power BI embed URL:**
1. Open your report in **Power BI Service** (app.powerbi.com)
2. Click **File → Embed report → Publish to web**
3. Copy the URL from the iframe `src` — it looks like `https://app.powerbi.com/view?r=eyJ...`
4. Paste it into `PBI_URLS` in `app.py`
